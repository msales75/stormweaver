Clients

Kurt Salmon Associates

 

content management, drag & drop outline sorting, internationalization, database design, custom search

 

Kurt Salmon is an international consulting company specializing in retail and health care. Their website involved links connecting many types of nested cross-references between many different types of content. They needed a custom content-manager to allow the company to maintain the content affordably. Working in conjunction with Chris Peterson, we developed a custom content-manager in 2008 to create a user-friendly interface for the editors to maintain the many types of dynamic content on their website. We significantly extended this program in 2009 with internationalization features.

Adams & Chittenden

 

content management, data structuring & migration, custom search, javascript freehand drawing widget

 

Tom Adams & George Chittenden run a scientific glassblowing shop in Berkeley that distributes custom glassware for laboratories around the world. Like many small businesses, they have built large website with many detailed pages, but which had grown rather unwieldy to maintain over the years.
